About Me

Hi, I'm Jaap, and I have a passion for developing elegant hardware.

For the past few months, I have been preparing to launch my first commercial hardware product on Kickstarter with startup Humans for Robots. So far I have, with mentorship, finished fully developing the product for optimal user experience and manufacturability and have travelled to Shenzhen, China, to set up supply chain and connect with some of the world's top experts in our industry.

At the same time I never stop working on side projects that allow me to experiment with new hardware and designs. Currently experimenting with lasers, cameras and Tensorflow for CSULB's Shark Lab.

This website is intended to be a place for me to document my projects and share any work I believe may be useful to others.
